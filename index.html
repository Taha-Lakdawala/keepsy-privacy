Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 July 2026

The short version
  • Your photos never leave your device. Keepsy sorts them entirely on your phone.
  • Your folders and how you organize photos are stored only on your device.
  • The only data we collect is your email address, and only if you choose to sign in.
  • We don't show ads, we don't use tracking or analytics, and we never sell your data.

This policy explains what information Keepsy ("we", "us", the "app") handles, why, and your choices. It applies to the Keepsy mobile app.

1. Information we collect

Photos and media — accessed on your device, never collected

Keepsy needs access to the photos on your device so you can view and sort them. This access happens entirely on your device. We do not upload, copy, transmit, or store your photos on any server, and we cannot see them. When you sort, move, or delete a photo, that action is carried out locally through your device's operating system.

Folders and organization — stored locally

The folders you create and your sorting activity are saved in a database on your device only. This information is not sent to us or anyone else. Uninstalling the app removes it.

Account information — only if you sign in

Signing in is optional; the core sorting features work without an account. If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive and store:

  • your email address, and
  • a Google account identifier used to authenticate you.

We use this solely to create and secure your Keepsy account. We do not receive your Google password.

What we do not collect

  • Your photos, videos, or their contents.
  • Your folder names or sorting history (these stay on your device).
  • Location, contacts, or microphone data.
  • Advertising identifiers or cross-app tracking data.
  • Third-party analytics or behavioral data.

2. How we use information

We use your email address and account identifier only to:

  • create your account and sign you in, and
  • keep your account secure.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the app.

3. How your information is stored

Photos and sorting data remain on your device. Account information (your email and account identifier) is stored using Supabase, our backend and authentication provider, on secure servers. Access is protected by industry-standard measures.

4. Third-party services

Keepsy relies on two third-party services, and only when you sign in:

  • Google Sign-In — to let you authenticate with your Google account. See the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase — to store and manage your account. See the Supabase Privacy Policy.

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing.

5. Data retention and deletion

We keep your account information for as long as you have a Keepsy account. You can remove your data at any time:

  • Photos and local data: uninstall the app, or clear its data in your device settings.
  • Account and email: email us at tahalakdawala03@gmail.com and we will delete your account and associated data within 30 days.

6. Children's privacy

Keepsy is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example under the GDPR, CCPA, or India's DPDP Act), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you, and to withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be communicated within the app where appropriate.
